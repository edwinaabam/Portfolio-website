Telecom Customer Churn Prediction

Python Scikit-Learn Machine Learning Streamlit Telecommunications

Overview

This project focuses on Reder Telecom, a major player in the telecommunications industry facing significant challenges with customer retention. By leveraging Machine Learning and Data Analytics, this solution identifies susceptible customers and provides actionable insights to mitigate churn.

The system utilizes historical customer data, including transaction history and service usage, to predict churn. This allows Reder Telecom to proactively address issues like network quality and pricing pressure before customers switch to competitors.

Key Goals & Features

  • Predictive Classification: Using Logistic Regression and Random Forest to predict churn based on historical data.
  • Marketing Efficiency: Optimizing efforts for targeted customer engagement and retention campaigns.
  • Revenue Protection: Safeguarding income by identifying and retaining high-value customers at risk.
  • Interactive Deployment: A user-friendly Streamlit interface for real-time churn prediction and visualization.

Telecom Churn Prediction Report

Prepared by: Edwina B Abam, Business Intelligence Specialist

1. Executive Summary

Reder Telecom operates in a highly competitive and dynamic industry. This analysis identifies that churn is primarily driven by intense pricing pressures, network performance issues, and evolving customer preferences. Retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones.

By leveraging supervised machine learning, we can identify patterns in customer demographics, purchase history, and service engagement. Implementing a data-driven retention strategy is expected to enhance customer satisfaction and provide a significant competitive advantage.

2. Key Factors Driving Churn

Network Issues: Poor quality and performance are direct contributors to customer dissatisfaction and subsequent churn.

Pricing & Competition: Numerous competitors offer similar services, making competitive pricing difficult to sustain while impacting profitability.

3. Data Structure & Methodology

Data Sources: The model processes diverse data types including Customer Demographics, Purchase/Transaction History, Subscription Service details, and Engagement/Feedback interaction data.

Preprocessing: We address class imbalance and perform feature selection to ensure the models (Logistic Regression and Random Forest) generalize well to new customer data.

Deployment: The solution is containerized using Docker and deployed via Streamlit to provide an interactive roadmap for stakeholders.

4. Monitoring & Visualization

To support ongoing strategic choices, the Streamlit application includes:

  • Trend visualization for prediction accuracy.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of usage patterns.
  • Real-time churn labeling for new customer entries.

5. Strategic Recommendations

Improve Network Infrastructure

Action: Prioritize technical maintenance and upgrades in regions showing high service-related dissatisfaction.

Goal: Enhance service quality and customer happiness.

Personalized Engagement

Action: Use purchase and interaction data to offer personalized service options that align with changing customer preferences.

Goal: Build long-term loyalty and increase additional purchases.

Proactive Pricing Adjustments

Action: Implement data-driven pricing strategies that balance competitiveness with profitability to stay ahead of market shifts.

Goal: Safeguard income and protect revenue streams.

6. Conclusion

Building long-term loyalty is a significant hurdle for Reder Telecom. However, by shifting to data-driven decisions and leveraging the predictive power of machine learning, the company can effectively target at-risk customers, optimize marketing efficiency, and significantly reduce the costs associated with churn.
